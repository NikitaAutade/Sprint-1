About Us

We Put People First.

Welcome to House Rental! House Rental is a disruptive real-estate platform that makes it possible to buy/sell/rent a house without paying any brokerage

House Rental was started because all of us believed that paying hefty brokerage can not be the only option to find a new home. As tenants, we have been paying these brokerages year on year without seeing any advantage of the broker. The only reason he existed was that there was a huge information asymmetry in the market. House Rental is a platform that removes this information asymmetry and provides a marketplace for free exchange of this information that used to cost 1-2 months of rent as brokerage.
